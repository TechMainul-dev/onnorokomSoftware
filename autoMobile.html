Automobile Inventory Management System

Inventory management system is a desktop/web application for tracking assets, orders, purchase, sales, issues and deliveries to avoid product overstock and outages/shortage. It can also be used in the manufacturing industry to create a work order, quotation, bill of materials and other production-related documents. It is a tool for organizing inventory data that was generally stored in hard-copy form with pencil and paper or in spreadsheets.

Common Purposes

Inventory management software is used for a variety of purposes, including:

  • Maintaining a balance between too much and too little inventory.
  • Tracking inventory as it is transported between locations.
  • Receiving items into a warehouse or other location.
  • Picking, packing and shipping items from a warehouse.
  • Keeping track of product sales and inventory levels.
  • Cutting down on product obsolescence and spoilage.
  • Avoiding missing out on sales due to out-of-stock situations.
  • Product depreciation calculation.
